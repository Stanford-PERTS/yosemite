Programs

Enrolling now for the 2014-2015 academic year!

Our programs are designed to raise academic achievement by changing the way students think about school. They are delivered over the internet and take 1-3 hours* of class time to complete. Some programs target students directly using brief online readings and activities. Other programs ask students to answer surveys and use the collected data to provide their teachers with research-based recommendations.
