Video Troubleshooting

If your videos will not play for you please try the following.

Check if you are blocked by a firewall

Try visiting youtube directly. If the page does not load, then your network is blocking youtube. Please talk to a network administrator or watch the videos on another network, like your home or a coffee shop.

Contact us

If you can access youtube, but not the videos on PERTS, then you have discovered some brand new problem (lucky you!). Please write to us at < or call us at (650) 241-9056 and we will help you to solve the problem.
